BIO

Ing. Karel Malec, Ph.D. has been working at the Department of Economics and Management at the Ambis University since 2021. In 2016, he defended his doctoral thesis focused on the development of prices and volatility of selected agricultural commodities. He continues to specialize in this field and publishes professional articles on the issue of agricultural commodity prices, trading, and their impact on developing countries at conferences and in international scientific journals.

Another area of his research is the evaluation of economic efficiency using financial analysis methods. Currently, he is involved in projects that map the impact of agricultural activities on the water balance in the Czech Republic, a project focused on young farmers and the impact of the Common Agricultural Policy for the new programming period 2021-2027, a project aimed at increasing awareness of sustainable practices for students of economic universities, and others..

In terms of teaching, he has extensive experience teaching in both Czech and English. In the context of teaching, he has collaborated with other universities in the EU (such as WUR Wageningen, FH Burgenlad, etc.).
